## 1. Montanema Handmade Village — Booking
🔗 https://www.booking.com/hotel/gr/montanema-handmade-village.html

- A "village" of **33 independent handmade stone lodges** over ~9 acres in the green Anthochori
  gorge, ~4.5 km from Lake Plastira. Bio-climatic stone-and-wood design.
- Each lodge has its own entrance, private courtyard/terrace and a kitchenette.
- Farm-fresh restaurant, café-bar, **Aquanema Spa, outdoor pool**, guided hikes (1–15 km) to the
  Anthochori waterfalls.
- **Price (10–12 Jul):** €790 total for 2 nights, 4 adults (incl. taxes & fees) → €395/night.
- Premium tier — the splurge option; book a lodge that sleeps 4.

## 2. Pefkofyto Mountain Retreat — Airbnb
🔗 https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/46405158
