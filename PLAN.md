# Weekend at Lake Plastira (Limni Plastira), Greece

## Context
A relaxed **food-and-villages weekend for 4**, driving from **Athens** on **Fri 10 → Sun 12 July 2026**
(2 nights, everyone together). The brief: stone-village charm, lake views, and good tavernas at an
easy pace — not a party trip. Lake Plastira (an artificial lake in the Agrafa mountains of Karditsa,
Thessaly, at ~750–800 m) is the ideal fit: cool mountain air in July, a 30 km panoramic loop road,
and a string of stone villages — **Neochori, Kalyvia Pezoulas, Pezoula, Kryoneri, Belokomiti** — full
of family tavernas serving local meat, pies and Messenikolas wine.

> Reality check: this is a genuinely cheap weekend versus the islands. The whole shortlist runs
> **€256–€790 for the 2-night stay** (4 guests). The plan below lands at roughly **~€650 (lean)** to
> **~€1,100 (comfortable)** all-in for 4, the stay being the main lever.

## Getting there (from Athens)
- **By car, ~3.5–4 h / ~290–310 km.** Athens → A1/E75 north, onto the **E65 (Central Greece motorway)**
  toward Lamia/Karditsa, exit for **Karditsa**, then up the mountain road to the lake (Neochori /
  Kalyvia Pezoulas). The last stretch is a winding but scenic climb.
- Fill up and grab any supplies in **Karditsa** before heading up — village shops are small.
- No need for a boat or ferry here (unlike the islands); the car is what makes the lake loop work.

## Day-by-Day Itinerary

### Fri 10 July — Drive up + first taverna night
- Late-morning departure from Athens; lunch stop near **Karditsa**.
- Check in at the villa/guesthouse, settle in.
- Sunset at a **lake viewpoint** (the Neochori/Kalyvia balconies over the water), then a long
  **taverna dinner** in Neochori — local meat on the grill, pies, Messenikolas red.

### Sat 11 July — The lake loop + villages (the centerpiece)
- Slow morning, village coffee.
- Drive the **panoramic loop around the lake**, stopping at viewpoints and stone villages
  (Pezoula, Kalyvia, Belokomiti, Kryoneri, Fylakti).
- Optional on the water: **kayak / SUP / pedal-boat** at the lake's activity centre, or hire
  **e-bikes** (Kazarma and others rent them) for a shoreline ride.
- Long **village-taverna lunch** somewhere with a view.
- Afternoon: the **Agrafa side** — a short forest walk (e.g. the Anthochori waterfalls near
  Montanema) if staying up there.
- Easy dinner back at base; tsipouro and dessert.

### Sun 12 July — Slow morning + drive home
- Late breakfast, last lake-view coffee, pick up local products (honey, pies, wine, trahanas).
- Midday drive back to Athens (~3.5–4 h), arriving evening.

*(If you want two fuller days, leave Athens early on Fri 10 and head back later on Sun 12 — the plan flexes.)*
